About Me!

My name is Stephanie Jarrett.

I have been playing the Violin since I was 7 years old. At the time that I began, I studied for a few years with Jenny Wadsworth and then following her instruction for a few more years with the late Jeanne Grover. I finished my studies through High School with Karen Child. Karen was able to take my ability to perform to places I never thought were possible. Even now I still look back in awe at what I was able to do!

I started teaching In 2015 when I was just shy of 21 years old. I started my teaching journey by helping out a couple of my cousins with their school music and what I thought at the time was just a side job/hobby turned into something I have a genuine love for! In the last year I have found a new love in playing the Viola! I started teaching myself back in the summer of 2022. A lot about learning the viola came naturally to me except for having to learn how to read Alto Clef! Since then, it has been such a fun experience to learn the different techniques the Viola requires compared to the Violin. I currently play with the Davis County Celebration Orchestra as a Violist and have been loving the experiences and joy I receive when performing with such a fun group!
